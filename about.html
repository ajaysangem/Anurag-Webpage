Anurag University, Hyderabad was established in the year 2020. The college is ranked 3rd among Best Infrastructure & Research Institutes in 2019. The institution offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the field of Engineering, Business Administration and Pharmacy. College is also known for offering a Ph.D. in Management, Technology, and Pharmacy. The college has a placement of INR 7.2 LPA in 2020 in a Management degree. Many corporates have carried placement drives for recruiting more than 3569 students. Corporates like Capgemini, HCL, TATA, Cognizant, Accenture, Infosys, IBM, DELL, HP, Axis Bank, Wipro, Netflix, Virtusa have visited Anurag University, Hyderabad campus for placements. Admissions are based on the merit rank of TS EAMCET conducted by the Telangana government.
